## Hexagram 1: The Creative

卦一：乾 — 创始之力

**Core Message EN:** This is not a moment to follow. This is a moment to initiate.

**核心信息 ZH：** 此刻不为追随，此刻为开创。

### Cosmic Timing | 天时

Pure yang is present. This is a rare window of creative force.
纯粹的阳性能量出现。这是一扇稀有的创生之窗。

You may feel an urge to act, to build, to express — and that urge is accurate. This reading does not promise ease, but it does confirm that the creative current is flowing with you, not against you.
你或许感到一股想行动、想建造、想表达的冲动——这冲动是准确的。此卦不承诺轻松，但它确认：创生之流正与你同行，而非逆你而去。

The warning is simple: do not confuse initiation with force. The power available now is like a river. Navigate it; do not try to possess it.
警告很简单：勿将开创与强迫混淆。此刻可用的力量如一条大河。驾驭它，莫试图占有它。

### Human Field | 人和

Others may look to you now — whether you asked for it or not.
他人此刻或将目光投向你——无论你是否主动要求。

This hexagram often appears when you are being positioned as a visible force. Some will admire you; others will project their own unlived potential onto you. Discernment is needed. Not everyone who applauds truly supports the work.
此卦常出现在你被置为可见力量之时。有人会仰慕你；有人会将自己未活出的可能投射于你。辨别力是必需的。并非每一个鼓掌的人都真正支持这事业。

Seek collaborators who are not in awe of you — people steady enough to question you without needing to diminish you.
去寻找那些并不仰望你的合作者——足够沉稳，能质疑你，却无需贬低你。

### Earthly Leverage | 地利

The leverage is momentum — but not scattered momentum. Directed momentum.
你的杠杆是势能——但不是分散的势能，而是定向的势能。

Choose one project that has been waiting for your full commitment. Give it the first hour of your day, before any reactive tasks.
选一个一直等待你全心投入的项目。把每天的第一小时给它，在任何被动回应任务之前。

Do not explain the vision too early to too many people. Protect its early form like a small flame.
不要过早向太多人解释这愿景。像保护小火苗一样，守护它的早期形态。

Surround yourself with symbols of clarity: clean lines, uncluttered space, direct language.
让自己置身于清晰的象征中：利落的线条、无杂物的空间、直接的语言。

You are not waiting for permission. You are building the container that permission will fill.
你不在等待许可。你在建造那个将被许可填满的容器。

## Hexagram 2: The Receptive

卦二：坤 — 承载之力

**Core Message EN:** Strength is not always a push. Sometimes it is a holding.

**核心信息 ZH：** 力量不总是推进。有时它是承载。

### Cosmic Timing | 天时

Pure yin is present. This is not a time to initiate, but to receive.
纯粹的阴性能量出现。此刻不是开创之时，而是承接之时。

You may feel pressure to act, to decide, to force something into shape. This reading gently suggests that the most powerful move right now is to wait — not passively, but with full presence. The soil receives the seed before the plant emerges.
你或许感到必须行动、决定、把某物强塑成形的压力。此卦温和地提示：此刻最有力的行动，是等待——不是被动的等待，而是全然出现的等待。种子入土，而后植株方出。

What comes toward you now matters. Pay attention.
此刻向你而来的，是重要的。留心。

### Human Field | 人和

You may feel overlooked, or like the work you do is invisible. This is temporary — and it may be protective.
你或许感到被忽视，或你所做的事不为人见。这是暂时的——且可能是一种保护。

This hexagram often points to a period where your role is more supportive than spotlighted. There is someone near you who needs your steadiness without knowing how to ask for it. You do not need to fix them. You only need to remain open and grounded.
此卦常指向一段你扮演支持性而非焦点性角色的时期。你身边有一个人需要你的稳固，却不知如何开口索取。你不必修补他们，只需保持敞开与扎根。

The right people will recognize your presence. Not by what you do, but by the quality of silence you bring.
对的人会认出你的存在——不是凭你做了什么，而是凭你带来的沉静质地。

### Earthly Leverage | 地利

Your leverage is receptivity — and this is not weakness. It is the capacity to let things ripen.
你的杠杆是接纳——这并非软弱。这是一种让事物成熟的容量。

Instead of initiating new things, finish absorbing something you have already started: a book, a conversation, a piece of unresolved grief.
与其开启新事，不如完成对某件已开始之事的吸纳：一本书、一场对话、一段未消化的悲伤。

Support someone else's creative act this week. Not with advice, but with practical presence.
这一周，支持他人的创造性行动。不用建议，用实际的在场。

Walk on actual earth. Barefoot if possible. The body needs to remember what receptivity feels like — without words.
真正走在土地上。赤脚，如果可以。身体需要忆起接纳的质感——无需语言。

The world does not always need another actor. Sometimes it needs a witness.
世界不总是需要另一个行动者。有时它需要一个见证者。

## Hexagram 3: Difficulty at the Beginning

卦三：屯 — 始生之难

**Core Message EN:** Everything begins in chaos. This is not a sign of failure.

**核心信息 ZH：** 万物始于混沌。这并非失败的征兆。

### Cosmic Timing | 天时

You are in the messy first stage of something. Nothing looks coherent yet, and that can feel like drowning.
你正处在某事的杂乱初始阶段。一切尚未成形，这感觉如溺水般窒息。

This hexagram describes the moment when a new thing — a project, a relationship, a version of you — is trying to push through the surface but hasn't broken through. The clouded confusion above (Water) and the trembling potential below (Thunder) are not against you. They are the texture of birth.
此卦描绘新生事物——一个项目、一段关系、一个版本的你——正试图冲破地表、却尚未破土的瞬间。上空是水云的混沌（上坎），下方是震颤的潜能（下震）。它们不是你的敌人，它们是诞生本身的质地。

You are not lost. You are still underground. The shoot knows which way is up.
你并未迷失。你仍在地下。幼芽知道哪个方向是上。

### Human Field | 人和

In the chaos of beginnings, helpers are often unrecognizable. They may come in forms that seem inconvenient.
在开端的混沌里，助缘常难以辨认。他们或以来得不合时宜的样貌出现。

This hexagram suggests someone near you is offering a kind of ordering energy — but their approach may feel too slow, too cautious, or too blunt for your urgent need. Do not dismiss them yet. They see a piece of the map you are too deep inside the problem to see.
此卦提示，你身边有人正提供一种秩序化的能量——但他们的方式对你迫切的渴求而言，可能显得太慢、太谨慎，或太直白。先不要推开他们。他们能看到一块你因身陷问题深处而看不见的地图。

Your task is not to follow them blindly, but to borrow their eyes.
你的任务不是盲从他们，而是借他们的眼睛一用。

### Earthly Leverage | 地利

Your leverage is patience with the mess — and one small ordering act per day.
你的杠杆，是与混乱共处的耐心——以及每日一个微小的秩序化行动。

Name one thing that is working, even a little. Write it down. The mind in chaos forgets what is already in place.
说出一样正在运转的事，哪怕很小。写下来。混乱中的心会遗忘已有根基。

Build one tiny container: a morning routine of 5 minutes, a folder for the scattered papers, a single repeatable phrase that calms you.
建一个小小的容器：一个五分钟的晨间惯例、一个收纳散纸的文件夹、一句可重复的、使你安定的短语。

Let someone help you with something small. Not a burdening request — just an open door.
让某人在一件小事上帮你。不是沉重的请求——只是一扇敞开的门。

The shoot does not push all the soil away at once. It moves one grain at a time.
幼芽不会一次推开所有泥土。它一粒一粒地动。

## Hexagram 4: Youthful Folly

卦四：蒙 — 蒙昧初启

**Core Message EN:** You do not need to know everything before you begin. But you do need to know what to ask.

**核心信息 ZH：** 你无需在开始前知晓一切。但你需知晓该问什么。

### Cosmic Timing | 天时

You are either the student or the teacher right now — perhaps both. There is something you genuinely do not know yet, and it bothers you.
此刻你要么是学生，要么是老师——或许两者兼是。有些事你确然尚未知晓，而这让你不安。

This reading suggests that the discomfort of not-knowing is not a flaw. It is the state that precedes real learning. Like a child who finally stops pretending to understand and asks the question that matters.
此卦提示：不知的不适并非缺陷，而是真正学习之前的必经状态。像一个孩子终于停止假装明白，问出了那个关键的问题。

Do not hide your confusion. It is the beginning of the path.
不要隐藏你的困惑。它是道路的开端。

### Human Field | 人和

Someone near you knows something you need. But the exchange is not flowing cleanly.
你身边有人知道你需要的东西。但交流未能顺畅流动。

This hexagram warns of two pitfalls: the teacher who insists on being asked too many times, and the student who pretends to already know. Look for a person whose style may not impress you, but whose knowledge is quiet and real. Approach them with one clear, humble question.
此卦警告两个陷阱：一个坚持要被反复请教的老师，与一个假装已经知道的学生。去寻找一个风格或许不令你印象深刻、但知识沉静而真实的人。带着一个清晰而谦逊的问题走向他们。

And if you are the one who knows: answer the question asked, not the one you wish they had asked.
而若你是知晓的一方：回答他们提出的问题，而非你希望他们提出的那个。

### Earthly Leverage | 地利

Your leverage is the beginner's mind — and one well-framed question.
你的杠杆是初学者的心——以及一个被好好框定的问题。

Identify the one thing you have been pretending to understand. Admit it to yourself, silently or out loud. Then seek clarity — from a person, a book, or a moment of honest searching.
找出那件你一直假装明白的事。对自己承认它，默然或出声。然后寻求清晰——从一个人、一本书，或一次诚实的探寻。

If someone asks you to teach, teach the simplest thing you know. Not the impressive part. The foundational part.
若有人请你教导，教你所知最简单的东西。不是令人印象深刻的部分，是基础的部分。

Spend ten minutes with something you are a beginner at. Let the awkwardness be there without fixing it.
花十分钟在你是一个初学者的事情上。让那种笨拙待在那里，不去修补它。

Not-knowing is not the same as being lost. It is the open space where new knowledge can land.
不知，不等于迷失。它是新知可以落地的空地。

## Hexagram 5: Waiting

卦五：需 — 待时之需

**Core Message EN:** The rain is gathering. Your task is not to make it rain, but to prepare the ground.

**核心信息 ZH：** 雨正在聚集。你的任务不是降雨，而是整地。

### Cosmic Timing | 天时

You are in a waiting period — but this is not empty waiting. This is the kind of waiting a farmer does after planting.
你正处在等待期——但这并非空洞的等待。这是农人播种后的那种等待。

Something you need (clarity, resources, an answer) is not yet available. It is forming, but it is above you — like clouds that have not yet condensed into rain. Your anxiety about the delay is natural, but pushing now would only exhaust the soil.
你需要的东西（清明、资源、一个答案）尚未可得。它正在形成，却还在你的上方——如尚未凝结成雨的云。你对延迟的焦虑是自然的，但此刻推进只会耗竭土壤。

This hexagram suggests nourishment while waiting. Eat well. Rest well. Stay simple.
此卦提示在等待中滋养自己。好好吃饭。好好休息。保持简单。

### Human Field | 人和

You may be looking for someone who can speed things up. That person does not exist right now.
你或许在寻找能加速事情的人。那个人此刻并不存在。

What does exist: people who can help you endure the wait with grace. This hexagram points to companions who are not fixers but sustainers — the friend who cooks with you, the colleague who does not demand false optimism, the elder who has waited before and knows the season turns.
存在的是：能帮你优雅地熬过等待的人。此卦指向那些不是修补者、而是维系者的同伴——与你一起做饭的朋友，不要求你虚假乐观的同事，经历过等待、知道季节会轮转的长者。

Do not isolate. Let small companionship hold the space that big answers cannot yet fill.
不要孤立自己。让小小的陪伴握住那巨大的答案尚无法填满的空间。

### Earthly Leverage | 地利

Your leverage is preparation — not pushing.
你的杠杆是准备——不是推进。

Identify what you will need when the waiting ends. Prepare it now, calmly: the documents, the clean workspace, the rested body.
明确等待结束时你将需要什么。现在平静地准备：文件、整洁的工作空间、休息好的身体。

Eat a meal slowly, without a screen. This is a ritual acknowledgment that you are alive and sustained — even now.
慢慢吃一顿饭，没有屏幕。这是一种仪轨式的确认：你活着，且被滋养——即使在此时。

If worry rises, name it: "I am worried about X." Then ask: "Is there anything I can do about X right now?" If not, return to preparation.
若忧虑升起，命名它："我在担心X。"然后问："此刻我对X能做什么吗？"若不能，回到准备中。

The farmer does not pull the shoots. The farmer waters.
农人不拔苗。农人浇水。

## Hexagram 6: Conflict

卦六：讼 — 争讼之诫

**Core Message EN:** Not every battle is yours to fight. Some are rivers you are meant to cross, not drown in.

**核心信息 ZH：** 并非每一场战斗都归你打。有些是你要渡过的河，而非溺于其中。

### Cosmic Timing | 天时

Conflict is present — and it is escalating. This reading suggests that forcing a resolution now would harm more than heal.
冲突正在出现——且在升级。此卦显示，此刻强行解决比等待会伤得更深。

The energies are imbalanced: Heaven above, Water below — two forces moving in opposite directions. You may feel pulled between principle and emotion. The reading does not say you are wrong. It says the current is too strong for a direct crossing.
能量正失衡：上乾天，下坎水——两股力量相背而行。你或感原则与情绪之间被撕扯。此卦未说你错了，它说的是此刻水流太急，不宜直接横渡。

Pause before pressing your case. The river will narrow upstream.
在推进你的主张前，先停顿。河流会在上游变窄。

### Human Field | 人和

Someone near you is in opposition — but not necessarily as an enemy. They may be carrying their own unspoken grievance, and you are merely the target it landed on.
你身边有人正对立——但不必然是敌人。他们可能背负着自己未被说出的委屈，而你只是那委屈落下的靶子。

This hexagram points to a third party — a mediator, an elder, a neutral observer — who can see what both sides are missing. Seek someone who does not need to win. Their presence alone can shift the dynamic from courtroom to conversation.
此卦指向第三方——一位调停者、一位长者、一位中立的观察者——他能看见双方各自遗漏了什么。去找一个不需要赢的人。他单单在场，就能将动态从法庭转向对话。

### Earthly Leverage | 地利

Your leverage is de-escalation — not because you are weak, but because the real win is upstream.
你的杠杆是降温——非因你软弱，而是因为真正的胜利在上游。

Write down your position in private. Get it clear for yourself. Then ask: "What part of this do I not need to say aloud?"
私下写下你的立场。为自己厘清。然后问："其中哪一部分我不必说出口？"

If a confrontation is unavoidable, meet on neutral ground — physically or symbolically.
若对峙不可避免，在中立之地见面——物理上的，或象征上的。

Do not make the other person lose face. Leave one door open, even if you do not think they will take it.
不要让对方失掉体面。留一扇门敞开，即使你认为他们不会走进去。

The sage who wins without fighting does not avoid conflict — he moves the battle to ground where resolution is possible.
不战而胜的智者并非回避冲突——他只是将战场移到可以化解之地。

## Hexagram 7: The Army

**Core Message EN:** To lead others well, you must first discipline your own attention.

**核心信息 ZH：** 欲善率众，先整饬己心。

In a situation that feels stuck, try the smallest possible new action. A different greeting. A changed routine. A single question you have never asked. Small shifts open large doors.
在一个感觉卡住的情境中，尝试最小可能的新行动。一个不同的问候。一个更改的常规。一个你从未问过的问题。微小的移动打开巨大的门。

Thunder shakes the mountain, but the mountain remains. The small actions you take with care, however, can reshape the terrain forever.
雷撼动山，而山依然。然而你以用心采取的微小行动，可以永远重塑地形。

## Hexagram 63: After Completion

卦六十三：既济 — 水火既济

**Core Message EN:** Completion is not the end. It is the moment before the next beginning. Celebrate it — then stay watchful. What is finished can unravel if neglected.

**核心信息 ZH：** 完成不是终点。它是下一个开始之前的时刻。庆祝它——然后保持警醒。完成之事若被疏忽，仍可瓦解。

### Cosmic Timing | 天时

A moment of completion is present. Water above, Fire below — the water boiling over the flame, the perfect balance of opposites. Something in your life has reached its culmination. A project, a phase, a struggle, a cycle. The finish line is real, and you have earned it.
一个完成的时刻正在出现。上水（坎）下火（离）——水在火上沸腾，对立面的完美平衡。你生活中某件事已达到顶点。一个项目、一个阶段、一场挣扎、一个循环。终点线是真实的，你已赢得它。

This hexagram carries a rare double message: success is here, and danger is also here. The moment of completion is precious, but it is fragile. The fire that boils the water can also evaporate it. The order you have achieved will not maintain itself. Completion is not a possession; it is a moment that must be actively tended.
此卦携带双重信息：成功在此，危险也在此。完成时刻是珍贵的，但它也是脆弱的。煮水的火也可以蒸发它。你已达成的秩序不会自我维持。完成不是一项财产；它是一个必须被积极照料的时刻。

### Human Field | 人和

You may be tempted to relax completely, and you should celebrate. But this hexagram warns against the assumption that the work is forever done. Some people around you may drift away now that the shared goal is achieved. Others may stay for the next chapter. Let the natural sorting happen.
你可能想完全放松，且你应当庆祝。但此卦警告勿以为工作永远完成了。你身边的一些人可能在共同目标达成后漂离。另一些可能为下一章留下。让自然的筛选发生。

This hexagram points to a companion of completion — someone who can celebrate with you genuinely, without envy. Their joy in your success is a mirror that shows you what you have actually achieved. Share the moment with them.
此卦指向一位完成的同伴——一个能真诚地与你庆祝、不带嫉妒的人。他们在你成功中的喜悦是一面镜子，显示你实际达成了什么。与他们分享此刻。

### Earthly Leverage | 地利

Your leverage is the celebration of completion — and the vigilance that follows it.
你的杠杆是完成之庆祝——以及随后的警觉。

Pause and acknowledge what you have finished. Not just in passing. Sit with it. Let yourself feel the weight of what you have carried and what you have now set down.
停顿并承认你所完成的。不仅是顺便。坐着与它同在。让你自己感受你所背负之重，以及你现在放下之轻。

Celebrate in a way that is meaningful to you. Not the celebration others expect. The one that actually feels like completion.
以一种对你有意义的方式庆祝。不是他人期待的庆祝。是那实际上感觉像完成的。

After the celebration, stay watchful. The fire that brought the water to boil must be tended, or the pot will cool. Completion is a state to be maintained, not a trophy to be stored.
庆祝之后，保持警醒。使水煮沸的火必须被照料，否则锅会冷却。完成是一种需被维持的状态，而非一件需被储藏的奖杯。

Fire below, water above — perfect balance. But balance is not static. It is a living tension that must be renewed.
火在下，水在上——完美的平衡。但平衡不是静止的。它是一种必须被更新的活张力。

### Ancient Root | 卦脉溯源

## Hexagram 64: Before Completion

卦六十四：未济 — 火水未济

**Core Message EN:** You are not finished. And that is not a failure. It is a sign that the story continues, and you are still in it.

**核心信息 ZH：** 你尚未完成。而那不是失败。它是故事仍在继续、而你仍在其中的信号。

### Cosmic Timing | 天时

A moment of incompletion is present. Fire above, Water below — the flame rising, the water sinking. They are not yet in balance. Something in your life is not finished. The final piece has not fallen into place. The answer has not arrived. The inner repair is not complete.
一个未完成的时刻正在出现。上火（离）下水（坎）——火焰上升，水下沉。它们尚未平衡。你生活中某件事尚未完成。最后一块还没到位。答案还没到来。内在修复尚未完成。

This is the final hexagram of the I Ching, and it is deliberately unfinished. The teaching is profound: life itself is a process of continuous becoming. You are not broken because you are incomplete. You are alive. The fire that has not yet boiled the water is still burning. The work is still underway.
这是易经的最后一卦，而它刻意地未完成。其教导是深远的：生命本身是一个持续成为的过程。你不是因为不完整而破碎。你是活着的。尚未煮开水的火仍在燃烧。工作仍在进行中。

### Human Field | 人和

You may feel that others have finished what you are still struggling to complete. This comparison is a thief of energy. The fire and the water in your life have their own timing. The people who truly support you are not the ones who demand your completion, but the ones who accompany your becoming.
你可能感觉他人已经完成了你仍在挣扎完成的事。这比较是能量的窃贼。你生命中的火与水有它们自己的时间。真正支持你的人不是那些要求你完成的人，而是那些陪伴你成为的人。

This hexagram points to a fellow traveler — someone who is also in the middle of something unfinished. Their presence reminds you that incompletion is not isolation. It is the shared human condition. Walk with them.
此卦指向一位同行的旅者——一个也处在意犹未尽之事中间的人。他们的在场提醒你，未完成不是孤立。它是共享的人类处境。与他们同行。

### Earthly Leverage | 地利

Your leverage is the willingness to stay in the process — to trust the unfinished as fertile ground.
你的杠杆是愿意留在过程中——信任未完成是肥沃的土壤。

Name what is unfinished, without shame. "I am still learning this." "I am still recovering from this." "I am in the middle of this." The truth spoken is a bridge to the other side.
不带羞愧地命名未完成之事。"我还在学这个。""我仍在从这件事中恢复。""我正处在这个的中间。"被说出的真相是通往彼岸的桥。

Take one step — just one — toward completion. Not to finish, but to continue. The fire that keeps burning eventually brings the water to boil.
朝完成迈出一步——就一步。不为完结，而为继续。持续燃烧的火终将水煮沸。

Trust the incompletion. The seed that has not yet sprouted is not dead. It is gathering. So are you.
信任未完成。尚未发芽的种子不是死了。它在蓄集。你也是。

Fire above, water below — the opposites not yet joined. But the fire has not gone out. The water has not stopped rising. The moment of balance will come. This is not the end. This is the middle. And the middle is where the living happens.
火在上，水在下——对立面尚未相接。但火尚未熄灭。水尚未停止上升。平衡的时刻仍有可能出现。这不是终点。这是中间。而中间是生命发生之处。
